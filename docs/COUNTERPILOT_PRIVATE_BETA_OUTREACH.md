# Counterpilot Private Beta Outreach

Use this as a starting point for direct merchant conversations. Keep it honest,
specific, and manual-first.

## Short DM

```text
I'm testing Counterpilot, a small Shopify make-an-offer flow for merchants who
want to approve offers manually and then see whether those negotiated orders
actually became mature margin after payment, refunds, and returns.

No AI negotiation, no conversion-lift claims, and no recovered-revenue claim.
The private beta is about handling the offer flow and reporting observed mature
margin cleanly.

Would you be open to a short look and telling me whether this would be useful
for your store?
```

## Slightly Longer Email Draft

```text
Subject: Manual make-an-offer flow for Shopify

I'm testing Counterpilot, a Shopify make-an-offer app for merchants who want to
keep offer decisions manual but still understand whether negotiated orders
become real margin after payment, refunds, returns, and maturity.

The current private beta is intentionally narrow:
- shoppers submit offers on selected products
- the merchant manually accepts, counters, or declines
- checkout is created only after buyer acceptance
- Counterpilot tracks payment, refunds, returns, and maturity
- the merchant gets an observed mature-margin report

It is not AI negotiation, not automated pricing, and not a conversion-lift or
recovered-revenue claim.

Would you be open to a short demo and a blunt reaction on whether this would be
useful for your store?
```

## Qualification Notes

Best early fits:

- Shopify merchants who already receive discount or negotiation requests.
- Merchants with meaningful product margins.
- Merchants who care about refunds and returns in margin review.
- Merchants willing to approve every decision manually during a private beta.

Poor fits:

- Stores looking for fully automated pricing.
- Stores that only need a discount-code widget.
- Stores that cannot provide margin assumptions.
- Stores expecting proven lift before any pilot.
